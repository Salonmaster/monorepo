# Welcome to SalonMaster 💈

SalonMaster is the Point of Sale system built for hair, nail, and beauty salons. Fast, simple, and works even when your Wi-Fi doesn't.

---

## What can SalonMaster do?

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

- **📅 Appointments**

    Manage your calendar, book clients, handle waitlists. Clients can book online 24/7.

- **💇 Client Management**

    Profiles, visit history, preferences, loyalty rewards — everything at your fingertips.

- **💰 Point of Sale**

    Fast checkout, multiple payment types, tipping, gift cards. Under 30 seconds from chair to paid.

- **📦 Inventory**

    Track products, manage stock levels, purchase orders. Know what you have and what you need.

- **👥 Staff**

    Schedules, commissions, permissions. Front desk sees different things than stylists.

- **📊 Reports**

    Daily summaries, revenue, tips, tax-ready exports. Know exactly how your salon is doing.

</div>
