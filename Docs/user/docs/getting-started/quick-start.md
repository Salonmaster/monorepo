# Quick Start

Get SalonMaster running in your salon in under 10 minutes.

---

## Before You Begin

You'll need:
- A computer or tablet with a web browser (Chrome, Safari, or Edge)
- Your salon name and address
- At least one service to offer (haircut, color, manicure, etc.)

That's it. No software to install.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [app.salonmaster.dev](https://app.salonmaster.dev)
2. Click **Create Account**
3. Enter your email and choose a password
4. Check your email for the verification link

![Signup screen](../assets/screenshots/signup.png)

---

## Step 2: Set Up Your Salon

After verifying your email, you'll land on the setup wizard:

1. **Salon name** — what clients see
2. **Address** — for your invoices and online booking map
3. **Phone** — client-facing contact number
4. **Currency** — EUR, USD, GBP, etc.
5. **Tax rate** — VAT/sales tax percentage (default 21% for EU)

!!! tip "You can change all of this later"
    Settings → Salon Profile

---

## Step 3: Add Your First Service

Services are what clients book. Examples: "Women's Haircut", "Balayage", "Gel Manicure".

1. Go to **Settings → Services**
2. Click **Add Service**
3. Fill in:
    - **Name** — what clients see when booking
    - **Duration** — how long it takes (e.g., 45 min)
    - **Price** — what you charge
    - **Category** — Hair, Nails, Beauty, etc.
    - **Color** — for the calendar (helps stylists see at a glance)

Add at least one service to continue.

---

## Step 4: Add Yourself as Staff

Even if it's just you, SalonMaster needs to know who's doing the work.

1. Go to **Staff → Add Staff**
2. Enter your name, email, and role (Owner)
3. Set your working hours (you can copy to all days if you want)

---

## Step 5: Book Your First Appointment

Let's make sure everything works:

1. Go to **Appointments → Calendar**
2. Click on an empty time slot
3. Search for a client (or create "Walk-in Test Client")
4. Select a service
5. Click **Book**

🎉 You're live! Walk through a full checkout to see the flow: Appointment → Checkout → Payment → Receipt.
