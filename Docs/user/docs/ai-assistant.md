# AI Assistant 🤖

SalonMaster has a built-in AI assistant that helps you run your salon faster. Talk to it like you'd talk to a coworker.

---

## What It Can Do

### 💬 Chat Commands

Type naturally — no special syntax:

| You say... | AI does... |
|---|---|
| "How much revenue did we do today?" | Queries today's transactions, shows total + tips |
| "Book Emma for a balayage with Sarah next Tuesday at 2pm" | Creates the appointment |
| "What's Marco's schedule tomorrow?" | Shows all of Marco's appointments |
| "Look up client Jane Smith" | Finds Jane's profile, history, preferences |
| "What services do we offer?" | Lists your salon's service menu |
| "How do I process a refund?" | Shows step-by-step refund instructions |

---

## Where to Use It

### 📱 In the App (Staff)

Open the AI chat panel:
- **Desktop**: Click the 💬 icon in the sidebar, or press `Ctrl+Space`
- **Mobile**: Tap the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner
- **Voice** *(coming soon)*: "Hey SalonMaster..."

The AI sees your salon's data — appointments, clients, revenue — and can act on it.

### 🌐 On Your Website (Clients)

Clients can chat with SalonMaster AI on your booking page:
- "Do you do gel nails?"
- "What's the price for a women's haircut?"
- "Is there parking nearby?"
- "Can I book for Thursday afternoon?"

The client chatbot answers from your salon info (services, pricing, hours) but doesn't access client data.

## Getting Help

Stuck on how to do something in SalonMaster? Ask the AI:

- "How do I set up online booking?"
- "How do I add a new staff member?"
- "How do I export reports for my accountant?"
- "How do I process a gift card payment?"

It knows the full help center and can walk you through anything.

---

## Privacy & Security

- The AI only accesses your salon's data — never other salons
- Client conversations on the website don't access your internal data
- Chat history is kept for 24 hours, then cleared
- No data is used to train external AI models
- All AI requests go through your SalonMaster backend
